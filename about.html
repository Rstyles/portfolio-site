About Me

Hi, I’m Ryan Styles. I'm a software engineer and coding instructor who’s passionate about helping others grow through code. I specialize in teaching core programming concepts in Python, C, and C#, and I focus on making complex ideas clear, approachable, and hands-on. Whether it's debugging student projects or whiteboarding game logic, I bring energy and clarity to everything I teach.

Outside the classroom, I enjoy building tools and experimenting with modern frameworks like Blazor, .NET, and WebAssembly. I'm always looking for ways to blend clean front-end design with solid back-end structure. When I’m not working with code, you can usually find me with a strong cup of coffee, customizing my Linux environment, or fine-tuning lesson plans to make learning more engaging.
